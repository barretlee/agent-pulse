# Copyright, Sources, and Responsible Use

Last updated: 2026-07-12

This document explains the copyright, attribution, correction, and responsible-use boundaries of Agent Pulse. It is an operational policy, not legal advice.

## 1. What the MIT License covers

Unless an individual file states otherwise, the repository's [MIT License](../LICENSE) covers Agent Pulse source code and original repository documentation created for this project.

The MIT License does **not** grant rights to:

- third-party articles, papers, filings, release notes, feeds, images, datasets, or software;
- third-party names, logos, trademarks, or product marks;
- text or media linked from an evidence record;
- Agent Pulse editorial analysis or exported intelligence content, unless that material is explicitly marked as MIT-licensed.

Third-party dependencies and vendored assets remain subject to their own licenses. See [Third-Party Notices](../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md).

## 2. Third-party source material

Source publishers and authors retain all rights in their material. A link, title, author name, publication date, identifier, or short excerpt is provided for attribution, evidence navigation, and research context. Inclusion does not imply endorsement, partnership, sponsorship, or permission beyond the applicable source terms and law.

Agent Pulse does not claim ownership of third-party source material. It does not intentionally mirror complete articles, paywalled material, or restricted datasets.

## 3. Public-data minimization

The public repository snapshot and GitHub Pages output should contain only the information necessary to identify and evaluate a signal:

- canonical source URL;
- source and author identity when publicly available;
- title, publication time, category, and provenance metadata;
- a necessary short excerpt of no more than 320 characters;
- Agent Pulse's original event-level synthesis and analysis.

Raw collector payloads, full source text, private feeds, cookies, credentials, proxy endpoints, operator notes, and source samples are not public export fields. A source-specific license or written permission may allow a different boundary, but that exception must be documented explicitly.

## 4. Analysis and automated processing

Agent Pulse separates source facts, system inference, editorial judgment, prediction, and opportunity hypotheses. Automated extraction and analysis can still be incomplete, stale, or wrong. Confidence, heat, impact, and value scores are system estimates used for prioritization; they are not objective truth or a substitute for the original evidence.

Event analysis should be original synthesis. It must link back to canonical evidence and should not reconstruct a third-party work through a sequence of excerpts.

## 5. Corrections, attribution, and removal requests

If you are a source owner, author, subject, or reader and identify an error, attribution problem, broken source link, privacy concern, or copyright issue, use the [Content correction or removal form](https://github.com/barretlee/agent-pulse/issues/new?template=content-correction.yml).

Please include:

- the Agent Pulse URL or record identifier;
- the original source URL;
- the requested correction or removal;
- supporting evidence;
- whether you are the rights holder or are reporting on someone else's behalf.

Do not publish private personal data, credentials, confidential documents, or security-sensitive details in an Issue. For a vulnerability or sensitive disclosure, follow [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may correct attribution, shorten or remove an excerpt, hide a public record while it is reviewed, or retain a minimal audit record when required for provenance. A removal from the public site does not silently rewrite historical evidence relationships.

## 6. Trademarks and external links

Company, product, model, and organization names are used only for identification. All trademarks belong to their respective owners.

External links are provided for evidence and convenience. Agent Pulse does not control their availability, content, security, or later changes. Users should evaluate the terms and privacy practices of the destination site.

## 7. Responsible use and disclaimer

Agent Pulse provides research and decision-support information. It is not investment, financial, legal, procurement, compliance, security, medical, or other professional advice. Do not make material decisions solely from a score, summary, timeline position, or Scout hypothesis. Verify the linked original sources and consult qualified professionals when appropriate.

Model prices, availability, policy status, company information, and other time-sensitive facts may change after publication. The generated-at and verified-at timestamps describe the observation time, not a guarantee of current accuracy.

## 8. Citing Agent Pulse

When referring to an Agent Pulse analysis, cite the specific page, version or access date, and preserve its original evidence links. A machine-readable citation record is available in [`CITATION.cff`](../CITATION.cff).

When referring to an underlying fact, cite the original publisher rather than Agent Pulse whenever possible.
